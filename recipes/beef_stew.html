Beef Stew

Description

Stew, a super simple super easy recipe to make but it also just taste really good. Full of nutrition for developers!

Ingredients

  • celery
  • Carrots
  • potatoes/li>
  • beef
  • broth
  • whatever spices you want
  • flour
  • olive oil

Instructions

  1. cut vegetables
  2. add olive oil to pot and then throw in the meat and flour
  3. throw in cut vegetables
  4. throw in remaining ingredients and stir, make sure broth covers everything in the stew
  5. cook in slow cooker because its easy
