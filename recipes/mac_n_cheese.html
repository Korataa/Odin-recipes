Mac N Cheese

Description

Baked mac and cheese. It’s nearly the universal comfort food, and there are SO many variations! In fact, I almost didn’t post this recipe, as there are SOOOO many mac and cheese recipes available, and most people already have their favorite way of making it.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb. dried elbow pasta
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • 1/2 cup all purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 cups whole milk
  • 2 1/2 cups half and half
  • 4 cups shredded medium sharp cheddar cheese
  • 2 cups shredded gruyere cheese
  • 1/2 tbsp. salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1/4 tsp paprika

Instructions

  1. preheat oven to 325
  2. boil water then add pasta after boiled
  3. shred cheeses and together to mix
  4. melt butter sprinkle in flopur then add milk and half and half
  5. heat over med heat, whisk often until thickened to very thick
  6. remove from heat and stir in spices and 1 1/2 cups of cheeses
  7. in large bowl combine drained pasta and cheese sauce
  8. sprinkle last bits of cheese over the top then bake for 15 mins until cheese is bubbly and gooey
